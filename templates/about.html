About LungAI Detector

Our Mission

LungAI Detector is an advanced artificial intelligence system designed to assist healthcare professionals in the early detection of lung cancer from CT scan images. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge AI technology to improve diagnostic accuracy and save lives through early intervention.

Technology

Our system utilizes a sophisticated fusion model that combines multiple deep learning architectures for robust and accurate lung cancer detection. The key features of our technology include:

AI Detection

Advanced convolutional neural networks trained on thousands of annotated CT scans

Explainable AI

Grad-CAM and LIME visualizations to understand AI decision-making

Detection Classes

NORMAL

No signs of lung cancer detected

BENIGN

Non-cancerous abnormalities detected

MALIGNANT

Potential cancerous growths detected

Medical Disclaimer

Important: LungAI Detector is designed to assist healthcare professionals and should not be used as a standalone diagnostic tool. All results should be interpreted by qualified medical practitioners in the context of comprehensive patient evaluation. Early detection through AI-assisted analysis can significantly improve outcomes, but final diagnosis and treatment decisions must be made by licensed medical professionals.
